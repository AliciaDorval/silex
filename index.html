I am a native New Yorker living a charmed life with my husband Richard and our two cats in Hastings on Hudson, a beautiful rivertown just outside of NYC.

My hobbies include cooking plant-based meals using produce from our CSA, experimenting with homemade natural beauty and personal care products, downsizing (my dream is to one day live in a tiny house), and crafting with up-cycled materials. I also enjoy organizing clothing, food, and book swaps because what's better than combining community and sustainability? I love sharing my passion for all these things on my blog.

Doodling, daydreaming, writing, and reading fiction have been my favorite things since birth. I am newly obsessed with performing improv. I am also an avid hugger.

Spirituality is a huge and wonderful part of my life. I practice Nichiren Buddhism with SGI, an organization striving for world peace. We believe each person has the power to overcome all of life's challenges, to live a life of value, and become a positive influence in their community, society, and the world. My faith motivates me to use my unique gifts to inspire and help others reclaim their power.

I have a BA in Writing from Hunter College where I also minored in Studio Art. I was trained as a health coach at Institute for Integrative Nutrition.
